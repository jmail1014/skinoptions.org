How to use this site

This website was set up for you. It is here for you to gain knowlege and solutions in the way of Esthetic procedures to solve your skin's challenges and reach your skin's goals. Please explore this site based on your individual needs.

The challenges and goals pages provide various options to choose from to discover which treatment is right for you. Check out the treatments page to look at all of the availible treatment options that your Esthetician can provide you. The schedule page will help give you a better idea of what you can do at home as well as with your esthetician or more advanded treatment options and their timeline.

If you have any additional concerns or questions use the contact me page to reach out.



About the Author

Hi, I'm Jessica. I have been an Esthetician for 14 years. My specialties are working with the epidermis of the skin (the outer most layer). This includes working with clients to achieve their ideal skin and helping to correct common challenges with skin treatments and at home skincare regimens. I believe that a little help and small shifts in routines are all a person needs to be able to reach any desirable skin goal.
